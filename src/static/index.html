RMOB meteor detections by year

Daily counts of meteor strikes recorded by paticipating members of the Radio Meteor Observing Bulletin, arranged by year in an attempt to show distribution accross the orbit of the Earth. Approxiate dates for the peaks of known showers are marked around the edge of the year circle.

The data is taken from selected RMOB observers, who have made their results available on the Visual RMOB Archives under the Creative Commons licence.
